The Real Problem

Why You're Not Getting
Interview Calls

If you've been applying for weeks with no response, the problem isn't your experience — it's your strategy. Here's what's silently killing your job search.

ATS Rejection Before a Human Sees You

Over 75% of CVs are auto-rejected before a recruiter ever reads them. If your CV isn't built for ATS, you're invisible — no matter how strong your experience is.

Applying to the Wrong Roles

Scattergun applications don't work. Without a clear target profile and role alignment, you're competing in categories where you're under or over-qualified. Both get ignored.

Zero Recruiter Visibility

70% of jobs are filled through recruiter networks before ever being posted publicly. If recruiters can't find you, you're missing the majority of the market.

No Strategy — Just Hoping

Applying on Naukri and LinkedIn and waiting is not a strategy. Without direct recruiter outreach and a targeted campaign, you're leaving interview calls entirely to chance.
