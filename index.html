The Process

6 Steps from Profile
to Interview Invite

A structured system — not random applications. Every step is designed to maximise your chances of getting shortlisted.

01

30-Minute Discovery Call

A focused call to understand your background, goals, target roles, and exactly what's stopping you from getting interview calls.

02

In-Depth Profile Analysis

We analyse your experience, skills, and current CV to identify gaps and determine the most strategic positioning for your target role.

03

Job Market Analysis & Report

We research demand, roles, companies, and salary bands — and send you a full report before we proceed. No surprises.

04

ATS-First CV Strategy

Your CV is rebuilt — not edited — with role-specific keywords, impact metrics, and formatting engineered to pass ATS filters and impress recruiters.

05

5+ Strategies for Interview Invites

Not just portals. Direct recruiter outreach, hidden job market, referral networks, LinkedIn optimisation — all personalised to your profile.

06

Interview Coaching

Mock interviews, answer frameworks, and confidence coaching to maximise your conversion rate once you're in the room.
